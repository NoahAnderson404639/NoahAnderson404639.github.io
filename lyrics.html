    This line make us remember to 2017 how things were before covid. It also make us refelct on the present and how much time we waste.

    "We're too busy and can't find the time"
    This is a very short but powerful verse. We always say we are so busy and can't find the time, but we spend most of it on our phones. This line convicts the intended audience and make them rethink how they are using their time.

    Josiah Queen using logos to connect with his audience is effective. The verses saying that we are too busy make the intended audience and himself reflect of the wasted time and how they can change their ways.
