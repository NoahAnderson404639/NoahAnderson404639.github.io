Lyrics

Link to the lyrics of Dusty Bibles

    Logos


    "I miss the way things used to be back in 2017"
    This line make us remember back to 2017 and how things were before Covid 19 and the addiction our phones are in our lives today. It also makes us reflect on the present and how much time we waste.

    "We're too busy and can't find the time"
    This is a very short but powerful verse. We always say we are so busy and can't find the time, but we spend most of it on our phones. This line convicts the intended audience and makes them rethink how they are using their time.

    Josiah Queen using logos to connect with his audience is effective. The verses saying that we are too busy makes the intended audience and himself reflect on the wasted scrolling on their phones time and how they can change their ways to be more intentional with their time.

    Pathos


    "We got dust on our bibles, brand-new iPhones"
    This line make us feel resentment for the way we have been using our time. "We got dust on our bibles" represents how long we have gone since reading our bibles while we spend time and money on new iPhones and being glued to them

    "And I just miss my friend Oh, I just miss my friend"
    In this verse it symbolizes that he has not talked to his friend in a long time. This action make him sad and make us sad as we realize and reflect on our life and how we have dust on our bible and we have not talked to Jesus in a while.

    Josiah Queen using pathos to connect with his audience is effective. The lyrics of his song makes the intended audience feel sadness guilty for not connecting with Jesus.
