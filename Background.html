Background

    Dusty Bibles is written by Josiah Queen. Josiah Queen is the speaker in the video telling the audience, which is people of the younger generation, about how he wants us to lose the phone and focus on what is important. The occasion is that in our day and age we are so distracted by our phones, he even realized he was not reading the bible as much as he should. The purpose of this song is to remind himself, along with others of this generation, how important reading the bible is, and the freedom it can bring. Though this song is about how we have walked away from faith, the tone is very happy and freeing. That even though we did walk away, Jesus never walked away from us.
